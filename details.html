IN THIS ROLE YOU WILL BE RESPONSIBLE
  • To plan, design, implement and execute various capacities planning and upgradations required in the IP network. Reporting and management of change management if work orders along with the general shift duty related to operations and maintenance of the core network.


HOW CAN YOU EXPRESS YOUR TALENT
  • Compliance to change management Procedure PIB-004 - Change activities in the IP network should be documented as per PIB-004, work orders maintained and all changes to be evaluated and vetted by change owner`s supervisor to ensure fault free service to customers.
  • Configurational network management
  • Network Operational Optimization
  • Ticket Management and root cause identification
  • Network automation management



WHAT YOU NEED TO BE SUCCESSFUL


Qualification
  • Bachelor’s degree in engineering is required; a master’s degree is highly preferred
  • Relevant certification like HCIP will be an added advantage
  • 6-8 years of experience in ISP, Data communications or telecom industry
  • Expert knowledge in IP routing protocols ISIS, BGP, OSPF, SR, SR-TE, PIM
  • Expert knowledge in MPLS routing protocols LDP, RSVP
  • Expert knowledge in Ethernet standards and technologies STP, MSTP, LACP, RSTP
  • Expert knowledge in Switching and VoIP system


Competencies:
  • IP Protocols
  • MPLS Technology
  • HCIP
